Test different streaming formats with the Universal Media Streamer

HLS Streaming

HTTP Live Streaming for adaptive bitrate playback. Supported by most modern browsers.

DASH Streaming

Dynamic Adaptive Streaming over HTTP. Industry-standard for high-quality streaming.

Direct Video

Direct MP4, WebM, MKV links. Simplest format - just open and play.
